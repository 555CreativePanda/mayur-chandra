Privacy Policy – Sudoku Infinity

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

App Name: Sudoku Infinity

Developer: Mayur Chandra

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. Sudoku Infinity is an offline-first Sudoku game that does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app runs entirely on your device without connecting to the internet.

2. What Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage or behavior tracking
  • Any information that could be used to identify you

3. Local Device Storage

The only data stored by the app is your game progress and settings, which are:

  • Saved locally on your device using secure storage
  • Never shared or transmitted
  • Automatically deleted if you uninstall the app

4. Third-Party Services

Sudoku Infinity does not use any third-party services, SDKs, ad networks, or analytics providers.

5. Children's Privacy

This game is safe for players of all ages. It does not contain inappropriate content or any functionality that collects data from users.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may occasionally update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the app update notes or this document.
